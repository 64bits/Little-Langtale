One day, Kenta, a university student, accidentally found an old map at a used bookstore. It was hand-drawn and seemed to indicate a hidden place deep within the forest behind the mountain. Driven by curiosity, he relied on the map and ventured into the forest the next day.

As he went deeper into the forest, the path gradually became steeper, but following the map's instructions, he reached an old stone hut. The inside of the hut was dim and covered in dust, but he saw a small wooden box placed in a corner. When Kenta opened the box, it contained not treasure, but several old photographs and a mysterious stone emitting a warm light. It was an irreplaceable discovery that enriched his heart.
